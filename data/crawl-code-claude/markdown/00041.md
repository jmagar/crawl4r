FAQ
### Who is eligible for the Anthropic for Startups Program?
The program is available to startups working with Anthropic’s VC partners. To qualify, your company must be an early-stage startup backed by one of our partner venture capital firms.
### How do I know if my VC is an Anthropic partner?
We recommend checking with your investors directly to determine if they are Anthropic partners.
### How do I apply for the Anthropic for Startups Program?
Reach out to your investors to get their unique program application link.
### What types of events are available to program participants?
Program participants receive exclusive invitations to startup-focused events, including workshops, webinars, and networking opportunities designed specifically for AI founders.
### What educational resources are provided?
The program offers access to educational resources tailored for AI founders, including best practices for implementing AI, technical documentation, case studies, and implementation guides.
### How does the program facilitate access to the Anthropic team?
Participants gain opportunities to connect with Anthropic team members through office hours, community events, and direct communication channels for technical support and guidance.
### Can I build commercial products with Claude through this program?
Yes, the program is specifically designed to help startups build and scale commercial applications using Claude’s capabilities.
### What happens after my credits are used?
After you’ve used your allocated credits, you can transition to standard API pricing. Program participants may receive guidance on optimizing usage and costs as they scale.
