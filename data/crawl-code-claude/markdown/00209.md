FAQ
### What data will Anthropic have access to?
When you prompt Claude in Slack, Anthropic gets access to your prompt and contextually relevant Slack conversation data that you can see. Anthropic retains your Slack conversation data for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes and criteria outlined in our [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy) or other agreement you’ve entered with Anthropic. Users and administrators can disconnect the Claude app at any time.
### How will my data be used?
Anthropic retains your Slack conversation data and personal data for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes outlined in our [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy) or other agreement you’ve entered with Anthropic. Anthropic doesn’t use conversations you have with Claude in Slack to train models.
### How much does it cost to include Claude in the Slack app?
Anthropic doesn’t charge anything additional to access the Claude App for Slack, but ordinary pricing and usage limits apply. Note: The Claude app for Slack is currently only available to install for paid Slack plans.
### What are the known limitations of Claude in the Slack app?
Research, file creation and editing, and extended thinking will not be available for Claude in Slack to start. Remember Claude can make mistakes, so please double-check responses.
### How should bugs and feedback be reported?
Thank you for helping us improve Claude. Please send feedback and bugs through our [support center](https://support.claude.com/en/).
### What do I need to know to get started?
Claude in Slack is available for paid Slack plans. A Slack admin must first approve the Claude app for their organization from the Slack App Marketplace in order for their employees to access the app from their workspace. From there, individuals can authenticate with their Claude login information.
You must have a [Team](https://claude.com/pricing/team) or [Enterprise](https://claude.com/pricing/enterprise) plan to use the Slack connector and bring Slack context to Claude. Before you can set up the Slack connector, you’ll need to install the Claude in Slack app.
### How does Claude in the Slack app work with Claude Code?
When Claude in the Slack app detects that you’re asking about a coding-related task, it will automatically relay the request to [Claude Code](https://claude.com/product/claude-code) to start a remote session on [claude.com/code](https://claude.com/code). This feature is available to users who also have access to Claude Code on the web.
